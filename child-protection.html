Complete Child Protection Policy

Last Updated: January 2026 | Version 3.2

1. Policy Statement

Baobab Digital Academy is committed to creating and maintaining a safe environment for all children in our programs. We have zero tolerance for any form of abuse, neglect, or inappropriate behavior towards children. This policy applies to all staff, tutors, volunteers, and anyone associated with our organization.

2. Definitions of Abuse

We recognize and respond to the following forms of abuse:

  • Physical Abuse: Any non-accidental physical injury to a child
  • Emotional Abuse: Persistent emotional maltreatment causing severe adverse effects
  • Sexual Abuse: Any sexual activity with a child, including online exploitation
  • Neglect: Persistent failure to meet a child's basic physical or psychological needs
  • Online Abuse: Any inappropriate behavior in digital environments

3. Prevention Measures

Our prevention strategy includes:

  • Comprehensive background checks for all staff and tutors
  • Mandatory child protection training for all team members
  • Clear codes of conduct for appropriate behavior
  • Regular supervision and monitoring of all interactions
  • Secure technology platforms with built-in safeguards

4. Reporting and Response

All concerns must be reported immediately to our Child Protection Officer. We maintain detailed records of all incidents and work closely with parents and relevant authorities to ensure appropriate action is taken.

5. Support for Children and Families

We provide ongoing support to children and families affected by child protection concerns, including access to professional counseling services and continued educational support during any investigation or recovery process.
