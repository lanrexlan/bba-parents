Our Tutor Verification Promise

Only the most qualified, passionate, and trustworthy educators become Baobab tutors. This is our non-negotiable commitment to your child's safety and success. Follow our rigorous 5-step process below.

Step 1: Application & Initial Screening

The journey begins with a comprehensive application. We review each candidate's background, teaching philosophy, and cultural competence. Only those who demonstrate a genuine passion for education and heritage proceed.

Step 2: Document & Qualification Verification

We leave no stone unturned. Our team meticulously verifies every document, including government-issued IDs, educational degrees, and teaching certifications, checking them against official databases in Nigeria.

Step 3: Background & Reference Checks

Safety is paramount. We conduct thorough background checks and contact multiple professional references to build a complete picture of the candidate's character, work ethic, and reliability.

Step 4: The Safeguarding Interview

A crucial, in-depth interview focuses entirely on child protection. We use scenario-based questions to assess a candidate's understanding of online safety, professional boundaries, and our strict code of conduct.

Step 5: The Live Teaching Audition

The final step is a live, observed teaching session. We evaluate their ability to engage, inspire, and effectively manage an online classroom, ensuring they meet the high standards your child deserves.

Our Promise is Our Foundation

This exhaustive process ensures that when your child logs in for a lesson, they are met by a tutor who is not only an expert in their field but is also a safe, trusted, and inspiring mentor. Your peace of mind is the benchmark of our success.
