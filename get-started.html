Getting Started Guide

Welcome to the Baobab Academy family! Here’s your simple, step-by-step guide to setting up and making the most of your Parent Portal.

1

Create Your Secure Parent Account

Your journey begins with a secure account. Only parents with an authorized email from our admissions team can register, ensuring a safe environment for everyone.

  • Click the 'Register' button on the homepage.
  • Fill in your full name, authorized email, and a secure password.
  • Check your email inbox for a verification link to activate your account.
2

Log In and Explore Your Dashboard

Once your account is verified, log in to access your personalized dashboard. This is your central hub for all information related to your child's education.

  • View your child's upcoming schedule at a glance.
  • See recent announcements from tutors and the academy.
  • Quickly access key features like progress reports and communication tools.
3

Track Academic & Cultural Growth

Our portal provides detailed insights into every aspect of your child's learning. Dive into the 'Progress' section to see their growth in real-time.

  • Monitor grades, assignment scores, and tutor feedback.
  • Follow their progress in cultural heritage subjects.
  • View visual charts that highlight strengths and areas for focus.
4

Connect Directly with Tutors

Open communication is key. Use our secure, built-in tools to stay connected with your child's dedicated, Nigeria-based tutors.

  • Use the secure messaging system for quick questions.
  • Schedule video calls for in-depth progress discussions.
  • Receive and respond to regular updates and feedback.

You're All Set!

You're now ready to be an active partner in your child's flourishing journey with Baobab Online Academy. If you have any questions, our support team is always here to help.
