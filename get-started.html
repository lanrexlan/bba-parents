Getting Started with Baobab Parent Portal

Everything you need to know to begin your child's educational journey with confidence. From account setup to celebrating their first achievements - we'll guide you every step of the way.

Home / Parent Resources / Getting Started Guide

Account Setup & Registration

Your first steps to joining the Baobab family and creating a secure environment for your child's learning journey.

1

Email Authorization

Before creating your account, ensure your email has been authorized by our admissions team. This security measure ensures only verified families can access our portal.

What you need:

  • Authorized email address from Baobab Academy
  • Access to your email inbox for verification
  • Contact information for your family
2

Create Your Parent Account

Complete the registration process with your family details. This creates your secure access to monitor and support your child's educational journey.

Registration steps:

  • Fill in your full name and contact details
  • Create a strong, secure password
  • Verify your email address
  • Set up your security preferences
3

Child Profile Setup

Add your child's information to personalize their learning experience and help us match them with the perfect Nigeria-based educator.

Profile information needed:

  • Child's name, age, and grade level
  • Current academic interests and strengths
  • Cultural heritage and language preferences
  • Preferred learning schedule and timezone

Orientation & Assessment

Understanding your child's needs and matching them with the perfect educational approach and tutor.

4

Welcome Consultation

Schedule a personal video call with our education specialists to discuss your family's goals, values, and expectations for your child's learning journey.

Consultation covers:

  • Your family's educational values and goals
  • Child's learning style and preferences
  • Cultural integration preferences
  • Scheduling and availability
5

Academic Assessment

Your child will complete a comprehensive but fun assessment to determine their current academic level and identify areas for growth and celebration.

Assessment includes:

  • Mathematics and problem-solving skills
  • Reading comprehension and literacy
  • Communication and language skills
  • Critical thinking and creativity
6

Tutor Matching

Based on your consultation and your child's assessment, we'll match you with a certified educator from Nigeria who aligns with your family's values and your child's needs.

Matching criteria:

  • Educational expertise and qualifications
  • Teaching style and personality fit
  • Language and cultural background
  • Schedule availability and timezone

Beginning the Learning Journey

Your child's first lessons and how to make the most of the Parent Portal from day one.

7

Meet Your Tutor

Join your child for their first introductory session where they'll meet their new tutor and you'll learn how to navigate the portal together.

First session includes:

  • Warm introductions and ice-breaking activities
  • Portal navigation and technical setup
  • Learning goals and journey planning
  • Communication preferences and schedules
8

Monitor Progress

Learn to use your Parent Portal dashboard to track real-time progress, view lesson recordings, and celebrate every milestone in your child's growth.

Portal features you'll use:

  • Real-time progress dashboard
  • Lesson recordings and notes
  • Direct messaging with tutors
  • Achievement tracking and celebrations

Tips for Success

Expert advice from experienced parents and educators to help you make the most of your Baobab experience.

Create a Learning Space

Set up a dedicated, quiet space for your child's online lessons. Include cultural elements like Nigerian art or books to reinforce their heritage connection.

Establish Routines

Consistency helps children thrive. Work with your tutor to establish regular lesson times and review schedules that work with your family's lifestyle.

Stay Connected

Regular communication with your child's tutor helps identify challenges early and celebrate successes. Use the portal's messaging system frequently.

Celebrate Achievements

Acknowledge both big and small wins. Cultural celebrations of academic achievements help reinforce positive learning associations.
