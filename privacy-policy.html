Our Privacy Commitment

At Baobab Online Academy, protecting your family's privacy isn't just a legal requirement—it's a fundamental value that guides every decision we make. We believe that exceptional education must be built on a foundation of trust and transparency.

This privacy policy explains in clear, understandable terms how we collect, use, protect, and respect your personal information. We've designed our practices to exceed international privacy standards while serving our mission of educational excellence.

Last Updated: January 15, 2025 | Version: 2.1

Our Core Privacy Principles

These fundamental principles guide every aspect of how we handle your personal information and make privacy decisions throughout your family's journey with us.

Data Minimization

We collect only the information necessary to provide exceptional educational services. No excessive data collection, no unnecessary tracking, and no information gathering beyond what directly serves your child's learning.

Purpose Limitation

Your information is used exclusively for educational purposes and child safety. We never sell, rent, or share your data for marketing purposes or any activities unrelated to your child's education.

Informed Consent

We provide clear, understandable explanations of how your information is used. You maintain control over your data with granular consent options and the ability to modify permissions at any time.

Individual Rights

You have complete rights over your personal information including access, correction, deletion, and portability. We respect your choices and make exercising these rights simple and straightforward.

1 Information We Collect

We collect information that enables us to provide personalized, safe, and effective educational services for your child. Our collection practices are transparent, purposeful, and limited to what's necessary for educational excellence.

Personal Information

Information that identifies you or your child, including:

  • Names, email addresses, and contact information
  • Age, grade level, and educational background
  • Learning preferences and accessibility needs
  • Emergency contact information

Educational Data

Information related to your child's learning journey:

  • Academic progress and assessment results
  • Lesson recordings and educational materials
  • Learning goals and achievement milestones
  • Tutor feedback and progress reports

Technical Information

Data necessary for platform functionality and security:

  • Device information and browser details
  • Connection quality and session metadata
  • Security logs and access records
  • Platform usage patterns for improvement

2 How We Use Your Information

Every piece of information we collect serves a specific purpose in delivering exceptional educational experiences while maintaining the highest safety standards.

Primary Purpose: Educational Excellence

All data usage is designed to enhance your child's learning experience, ensure safety, and support academic achievement.

  • Personalized Learning: Tailoring educational content and teaching methods to your child's individual needs and learning style
  • Progress Tracking: Monitoring academic development and providing detailed progress reports to parents
  • Safety Monitoring: Ensuring appropriate interactions and maintaining child protection protocols
  • Communication: Facilitating secure communication between families, tutors, and support staff
  • Platform Improvement: Enhancing our services based on usage patterns and feedback
  • Compliance: Meeting legal requirements and maintaining educational standards

3 Information Sharing and Disclosure

We maintain strict controls over information sharing and only disclose data when necessary for educational services, safety, or legal compliance.

Authorized Educational Sharing

  • Verified tutors receive only information necessary for teaching your child
  • Educational support staff access data relevant to their role in your child's learning
  • Parents and guardians have full access to their child's educational records

Service Providers

We work with carefully vetted service providers who help us deliver our educational platform. These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and can only use information for specified purposes.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, to protect safety, or to respond to valid legal requests from authorities. We will notify affected families whenever legally possible.

What We Never Do

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We don't share data for marketing purposes or any activities unrelated to education and safety.

4 Children's Privacy Protection

Protecting children's privacy requires special care and enhanced safeguards. We exceed industry standards in our approach to children's data protection.

COPPA Compliance

We fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal information from children under 13.

Enhanced Protections

  • All children's data is encrypted with additional security layers
  • Access to children's information is strictly limited and monitored
  • Parents maintain complete control over their child's data
  • Automatic deletion protocols for unnecessary data
  • Regular audits of children's data protection measures

Parental Rights

Parents have the right to review, modify, or delete their child's personal information at any time. We provide easy-to-use tools for exercising these rights through the Parent Portal.

5 Data Security and Protection

We employ multiple layers of security to protect your family's information using industry-leading technologies and practices.

Technical Safeguards

  • AES-256 encryption for all data storage and transmission
  • Multi-factor authentication for all account access
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Automated threat detection and response systems
  • Secure, redundant data backup systems

Administrative Safeguards

  • Strict access controls based on job responsibilities
  • Regular security training for all staff members
  • Comprehensive background checks for all personnel
  • Incident response procedures and protocols
  • Regular reviews of security policies and procedures

Physical Safeguards

  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring
  • Biometric access controls for sensitive areas
  • Environmental monitoring and protection systems
  • Secure disposal of physical media and equipment

6 Your Privacy Rights

You have comprehensive rights regarding your personal information. We make exercising these rights simple and provide multiple ways to manage your privacy preferences.

Access Rights

You can access and review all personal information we hold about you and your child through the Parent Portal or by contacting our privacy team directly.

Correction Rights

If any information we hold is inaccurate or incomplete, you can correct it directly through your account or request corrections from our support team.

Deletion Rights

You can request deletion of personal information, subject to legal retention requirements. We'll delete data promptly and confirm the deletion with you.

Portability Rights

You can receive a copy of your personal information in a structured, commonly used format and have it transferred to another service provider if desired.

Objection Rights

You can object to certain uses of your information and request restrictions on processing for specific purposes.

7 Data Retention and Deletion

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for educational purposes, safety requirements, and legal compliance.

Retention Periods

  • Active Student Records: Maintained during enrollment and for 7 years after graduation for transcript purposes
  • Safety Records: Retained for 10 years for child protection purposes
  • Technical Logs: Deleted after 90 days unless required for security investigations
  • Marketing Information: Deleted immediately upon opt-out or account closure

Automatic Deletion

Our systems automatically delete information that's no longer needed, ensuring we don't retain data longer than necessary.

8 International Data Transfers

While our primary operations are based in Nigeria, we may transfer data internationally for specific educational or technical purposes, always with appropriate safeguards.

Transfer Safeguards

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for GDPR compliance
  • Adequacy decisions where available
  • Additional encryption for international transfers
  • Regular compliance audits of international partners

9 Policy Updates and Changes

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We'll always notify you of significant changes.

Notification Process

  • Email notification to all registered parents
  • Prominent notice in the Parent Portal
  • Summary of changes provided in plain language
  • 30-day notice period before material changes take effect
