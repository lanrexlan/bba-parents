Our Privacy Commitment

At Baobab Digital Academy, protecting your family's privacy isn't just a legal requirement—it's a fundamental value that guides every decision we make. We believe that exceptional education must be built on a foundation of trust and transparency.

This privacy policy explains in clear, understandable terms how we collect, use, protect, and respect your personal information. We've designed our practices to exceed international privacy standards while serving our mission of educational excellence.

Last Updated: January 15, 2026 | Version: 2.1

Our Core Privacy Principles

These fundamental principles guide every aspect of how we handle your personal information and make privacy decisions throughout your family's journey with us.

Data Minimization

We collect only the information necessary to provide exceptional educational services. No excessive data collection, and no tracking beyond what serves your child's learning.

Purpose Limitation

Your information is used exclusively for educational purposes and safety. We never sell, rent, or share your data for marketing purposes.

Informed Consent

We provide clear explanations of how your info is used. You maintain control over your data with granular consent options that you can modify at any time.

Individual Rights

You have complete rights over your personal information including access, correction, deletion, and portability. We make exercising these rights simple.

1 Information We Collect

We collect information that enables us to provide personalized, safe, and effective educational services for your child. Our collection practices are transparent, purposeful, and limited to what's necessary for educational excellence.

Personal Information

Information that identifies you or your child, including:

  • Names, email addresses, and contact information
  • Age, grade level, and educational background
  • Learning preferences and accessibility needs
  • Emergency contact information

Educational Data

Information related to your child's learning journey:

  • Academic progress and assessment results
  • Lesson recordings and educational materials
  • Learning goals and achievement milestones
  • Tutor feedback and progress reports

Technical Information

Data necessary for platform functionality and security:

  • Device information and browser details
  • Connection quality and session metadata
  • Security logs and access records
  • Platform usage patterns for improvement

2 How We Use Your Information

Every piece of information we collect serves a specific purpose in delivering exceptional educational experiences while maintaining the highest safety standards.

Primary Purpose: Educational Excellence

All data usage is designed to enhance your child's learning experience, ensure safety, and support academic achievement.

  • Personalized Learning: Tailoring educational content to your child's individual needs
  • Progress Tracking: Monitoring academic development and providing reports
  • Safety Monitoring: Ensuring appropriate interactions and protocols
  • Communication: Facilitating secure communication between families and tutors
  • Platform Improvement: Enhancing our services based on usage patterns
  • Compliance: Meeting legal requirements and educational standards

3 Information Sharing & Disclosure

We maintain strict controls over information sharing and only disclose data when necessary for educational services, safety, or legal compliance.

Authorized Educational Sharing

  • Verified tutors receive only information necessary for teaching
  • Support staff access data relevant to their specific role
  • Parents and guardians have full access to their child's records

Service Providers

We work with carefully vetted service providers who help us deliver our platform. They are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and use information only for specified purposes.

What We Never Do

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We don't share data for marketing purposes or any activities unrelated to education and safety.

4 Children's Privacy Protection

Protecting children's privacy requires special care. We exceed industry standards in our approach to children's data protection.

COPPA Compliance

We fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal information from children under 13.

Enhanced Protections

  • All children's data is encrypted with additional security layers
  • Access to children's information is strictly limited and monitored
  • Parents maintain complete control over their child's data
  • Automatic deletion protocols for unnecessary data

5 Data Security & Protection

We employ multiple layers of security to protect your family's information using industry-leading technologies and practices.

Technical Safeguards

  • AES-256 encryption for all data storage and transmission
  • Multi-factor authentication for all account access
  • Automated threat detection and secure data backup systems

6 Your Privacy Rights

You have comprehensive rights regarding your personal information. We make exercising these rights simple.

Access & Correction

You can access, review, and correct all personal information we hold about you and your child directly through your Parent Portal dashboard.

Deletion & Portability

You can request deletion of personal information or receive a copy of your data in a structured format by contacting our privacy team.

7 Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for educational purposes, safety requirements, and legal compliance.

Retention Periods

  • Active Student Records: Maintained during enrollment and for 7 years after graduation
  • Safety Records: Retained for 10 years for child protection purposes
  • Technical Logs: Deleted after 90 days
